History of the Day Family

Darian and Heather Day were married in 2017. They went on to have 3 children, Rylee, Ryen, and Darian Jr.

Details about Darian and Heather

Darian and Heather met back in 2006 in St. Louis, Missouri

It all began when two young professionals met one day in the middle of the street. A quick "welcome to the neighborhood" conversation evolved into an everlasting friendship and marriage...
After several months of long distance calls and over the weekend road trips, I decided that I would ask Heather to become my wife. So on a planned trip to Las Vegas, I took my chance to make it a memorable trip. In front of the Bellagio Hotel, after walking around with a ring box in my socks for several hours, I asked Heather to marry me!





And she said,"Yes!"



On June 7th, 2008, we became Mr. and Mrs. Darian Day!


And our union has been blessed with 3 beautiful and intelligent children.
Their names are Rylee, Ryen, and Darian "DJ" Jr. They make us laugh, they make us cry, and they make us so proud and we love them to death!



And on June 7th, 2018, we celebrated 10 years of marriage! We have had so memorable moments along the way. I could not have chosen a better person to spend the rest of my life with. I truly adore and love my wife and I for many more years with her!
